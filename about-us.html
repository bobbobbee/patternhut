About Us

We are first and foremost crafters - loving to create beuatiful, hand crafted items.  We love almost any type of needle craft - especially knitting, crochette and crossstich.  It is not unusual to find us in the yard section of the nearest Walmart or visiting yarn and craft stores as we travel.

We have made it out mission to collect and archive some of the best free patterns - some available directly from our website, many linked to the source.  Sharing is one of the things that keeps this hobby exciting and fun.

We hope you enjoy our website.  Drop us a line on the contact us page.

So who exactly are we?

I am Brad, I go by the handle Bjaz.  I am a semi retired software geek.  I run the website and enable my better halfs hobby.

Anna is my better half.  She is the crafter here.  She loves to tweek patterns she finds, and sometimes we will share those also.

God Bless!
